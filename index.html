01

Raw Material Selection

We source long-staple fleece directly from certified local collection centers, evaluating every lot for strength and purity.

02

Wool Processing

Fleece undergoes systematic eco-responsible cleaning, degreasing, and detailed combing to align structural fibers.

03

Fabric Manufacturing

Advanced high-speed automated weaving looms convert prepared yarns into tightly structured, premium fabrics.

04

Every single yard passes through high-precision analytical checks to catch structural inconsistencies, color drifts, or weave defects.

05

Specialized eco-safe washing, pressing, and finishing processes enhance tactile softness and maximize anti-shrink behavior.

06

Packaging

Finished goods are secured within multi-layer, moisture-proof vacuum barriers, preserving their condition for long-distance transit.

Through our seamless, fully tracked logistics network, we ensure reliable, on-time delivery across domestic and global B2B endpoints.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical minimum order quantity (MOQ) for custom blends?

+

Our standard wholesale manufacturing workflows typically require a baseline configuration of 1,500 meters per distinct colorway or custom structural matrix blend. This ensures optimal mechanical yield efficiency on our high-speed automated production lines.

How does your factory ensure standard color matching across separate production batches?

+

We leverage advanced spectrophotometer color matching technology alongside automated computer-controlled chemical measurement systems. This precise setup maintains variations well below noticeable thresholds, ensuring uniform consistency across seasonal orders.

Are your raw materials ethically and sustainably sourced within India?

+

Absolutely. Our Swadeshi product lines are verified through transparent, direct-purchase relationships with local agricultural cooperatives. We ensure fair compensation structures and focus on traceably harvesting seasonal sheep wool fleece.

What are your standard logistics timelines for cross-state delivery?

+

Once production is finished and the multi-layer packaging is complete, domestic freight shipments typically reach major Indian industrial hubs within 4 to 7 business days, depending on local transit routes.
